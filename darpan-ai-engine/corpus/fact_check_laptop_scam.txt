Source: PIB Fact Check
Date: September 18, 2025
Claim: A message circulating on social media claims that the Government of India is providing free laptops to all students.
Fact: This claim is FAKE. No such scheme has been announced by the Government of India. The links provided in these messages, such as 'bharat-laptop-yojana.in', are fraudulent and designed for phishing. Citizens are advised not to click on such links or share their personal information. Always verify government schemes on official websites.
